Grow Journal

My children, family, friends, and community need the best information about growing healthy food.

By recording our growing experiences we are able to communicate what we are doing so we are offered the opportunity to piece together our past, present and future.

But this valuable information is locked away on pages of books, files on computers, and random websites. None of which linked together or organized.

We know fixing this problem for like minded people with the help of computers will create a powerful technology never before seen.

Grow Journal is a platform that enables growers everywhere the ability to digitally log, store, analyze, and share their most valuable information and results.
